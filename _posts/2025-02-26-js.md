## Operator
---

- Truthy, Falsy
  - Falsy: `false`, `0`, `-0`, `""`, `null`, `undefined`, `NaN`
- 1 `==` "1" `true`
- 1 `===` "1" `false`
- `!=` `!==` `++` `--` `+=` `-=`
- `!!X` : boolean(X)
- `&&` `||` `!`
  > `"hello" && 0 = 0`
  >
  > `"hello" && "world" = "world"`
  >
  > `"hello" || null = "hello"`
  >
  > `"hello" || "world" == "hello"`
- `Condition ? Expr1 : Expr2`
  - If `Condition` is `true` then `Expr1`, else `Expr2`. 
- `A ?? B`
  - If `A` is `null` or `undefined`, then `B`, else `A`. 
- `...X`
  > `[...myArr1, ...myArr2]`
  - Unpacking(Array, String, Set, Map, Object)
